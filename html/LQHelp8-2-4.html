Find Tag¶

Enables a search bar at the top of the DICOM tag workspace. The filter bar can be enabled by clicking the button on the DICOM Tool Bar or by selecting “DICOM Tags’ from the Edit Menu. It can also be toggled on/off from the context menu. See DICOM Tool Bar for editing DICOM files.

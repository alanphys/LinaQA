Tomographic Uniformity Analysis¶

Calculates the integral and differential uniformity of a SPECT image or series of images. Images between the ‘First frame’ and ‘Last frame’ settings are averaged together and the analysis is done on the resulting image. The analysis can be opened by clicking the button on the Nuclear Medicine Tool Bar or by selecting ‘Tomographic Uniformity’ from the Nuclear Medicine analysis menu.

The ‘First frame’ and ‘Last frame’ as well as other settings can be set in Tomographic Uniformity settings.

Please see Tomographic Uniformity in the Pylinac documentation for more information.

Flip Up down¶

Swaps the top and bottom of the image or images. The image is then autowindowed The image can be flipped by clicking the button on the Radiology Tool Bar or by selecting ‘Flip Up Down’ from the Image Menu.

All the loaded images will be flipped, not just the currently displayed one.

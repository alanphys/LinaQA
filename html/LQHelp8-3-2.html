Invert¶

Swaps the image maximum and minimum and rescales the other values accordingly. The image is then autowindowed The image can be inverted by clicking the button on the Radiology Tool Bar or by selecting ‘Invert’ from the Image Menu. This is a non-destructive operation, i.e. it does not change the image data.

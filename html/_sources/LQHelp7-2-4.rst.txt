Pixel Data Tab
==============

The Pixel Data tab displays the currently displayed image as a row x column grid of pixel values. The tab can be opened as given in :ref:`editpixeldata`. Operations available on the tab are:

*  Mouse wheel scrolls up/down through the values.
*  Left mouse button click selects a cell.
*  Left mouse button double click enters cell for editing.
*  Left mouse click on the scroll bars to scroll left/right and up/down

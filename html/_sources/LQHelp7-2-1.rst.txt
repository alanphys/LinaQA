Image Tab
=========

The Image tab displays the currently open image or image series. The tab can be opened as given in :ref:`fileopen`. Operations available on the tab are:

*  Mouse wheel scrolls through an image series.
*  Vertical click and drag with the left mouse button will increase/decrease the window level.
*  Horizontal click and drag with the left mouse button will increase/decrease the window width.
*  Drag and drop image files onto the Image tab to open them.

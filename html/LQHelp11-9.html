Spatial ResolutionÂ¶

These contain customisable options for Spatial Resolution Analysis. Available settings are:

  • Bar widths mm: Comma delimited list of bar widths in mm for each quadrant. A line pair will be 2x this value. Applies to Quadrant Spatial Resolution Analysis

  • Distance from centre: The distance from the center of the image to the center of the ROI in mm. Applies to Quadrant Spatial Resolution Analysis

  • ROI diameter mm: The diameter of the ROI in mm. Applies to Quadrant Spatial Resolution Analysis

  • ROI width mm: The width of the ROI. This is the width in the parallel to the sample lines. Applies to Four Bar Spatial Resolution Analysis.

  • Resolution test: Default spatial resolution test.

  • Separation mm: The distance between the two peaks formed by the bars in x or y direction in mm. The length of the ROI to sample will be 2x the separation perpendicular to the sample lines. Applies to Four Bar Spatial Resolution Analysis.
